People & Employee Services

Psychology led solutions for people, employees and workplaces

At an organisational level, we support healthy workplaces – on an individual level, we help to optimise your mental health and wellbeing.

Find a service Company solutions

Whether you need a single service or a combination, we’re here to tailor a solution to support you

Employee Assistance Program

Our psychologists deliver tailored Employee Assistance Programs for organisations, to provide their employees and eligible family members access to free confidential counselling. Learn more

Critical Incident Response

The Critical Incident Response intervention provides psychological support for distressed employees or family members following a critical incident. Learn more

Movement and Work Conditioning Programs

We plan conditioning programs for employees tailored to their duties and work environment to ensure they can carry out their daily activities in a safe and healthy way. Learn more

Mediation

As psychologists, we’re able to apply our assessment, facilitation and reasoning skills to settle conflicts between parties, such as employers, employees and insurers. Learn more

Therapeutic Services (in person or virtual)

We provide Medicare and privately funded psychological counselling, physical treatment and pain management support, utilising evidence-based interventions. Learn more

Mental Health Treatment

Our psychologists apply evidence-based best practices and a range of treatment modalities to treat those living with an illness or issue impacting their mental health. Learn more

Every person and workplace presents unique challenges that require tailored solutions
As an integrated hub of professionals, we cover all aspects of mental, physical and workplace wellbeing. We bring a holistic, tailored solution to challenges to elevate wellbeing.
As an integrated team we’re able to cover all aspects of mental, physical and social wellbeing.
We employ empathetic professionals experienced in utilising evidence-based interventions.
We measure, predict and diagnose employee mental health and wellbeing issues, to tailor therapeutic services.
Our proactive approach identifies and addresses potential workplace issues before they present at the individual level.
We've worked as a trusted partner with employees, people and workplaces across private and public sectors for over 20 years.
We’re available through telehealth and face-to-face appointments at our offices located across the country.
